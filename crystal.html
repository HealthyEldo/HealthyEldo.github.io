CRYSTAL BASIN

Restoring landscape resilience in the Crystal Basin watershed through innovative forest management and community partnerships.

Learn More

Project Overview

The Crystal Basin project represents a flagship initiative in landscape-scale forest restoration. Located in the heart of the Sierra Nevada, this critical watershed provides water supply for millions of Californians while supporting diverse ecosystems and recreational opportunities. Our restoration efforts focus on:

Fuel Reduction

Strategic thinning and prescribed burns to reduce wildfire risk and restore natural fire cycles.

Ecosystem Health

Improving forest structure, wildlife habitat, and watershed function through science-based management.

Restoration Goals

Fire Management

Reducing catastrophic fire risk through strategic fuel treatments and prescribed burning.

Water Security

Protecting and enhancing water quality and supply for downstream communities.

Wildlife Habitat

Creating diverse habitats that support native wildlife species and biodiversity.

Community Benefits

Supporting local economies through sustainable forest management practices.
